Why Partner with Hint Mint?

Established Brand Identity: Hint Mint has cultivated a distinctive position in the premium confectionery market as a brand synonymous with quiet luxury and creative flair. Our iconic tin packaging and patented leaf-shaped mints are instantly recognizable, offering your customers a product that stands apart from the ordinary.

Premium Product Differentiation: In a crowded marketplace, Hint Mint offers genuine differentiation. Our products are 100% sugar-free, dairy-free, gluten-free, vegan, and Non-GMO Project Verified — meeting the growing demand for premium products that align with modern dietary preferences and health-conscious lifestyles.

Private Label Opportunities: Beyond our signature collections, we offer comprehensive private label services. Partner with us to create custom-branded mints that reflect your own brand identity, leveraging our manufacturing expertise and premium quality standards.

How Hint Mint Enhances Your Product Portfolio

Targeted to Upscale Clientele: Hint Mint is designed for discerning consumers who appreciate the finer things in life. By adding Hint Mint to your product lineup, you can attract and retain high-value customers who seek premium, thoughtfully crafted products.

Cross-Selling Potential: Our products complement a wide range of retail environments — from boutique hotels and high-end restaurants to luxury retail stores and specialty gift shops. Hint Mint tins serve as the perfect impulse purchase, gift add-on, or curated collection piece.

Sustainability Commitment: Today's consumers increasingly choose brands that reflect their values. Hint Mint's commitment to recyclable and reusable packaging, responsibly sourced ingredients, and charitable partnerships makes our products a compelling choice for environmentally and socially conscious buyers.
