Hint Mint is officially back in Japan!

After a few years away, Hint Mint is excited to return to Japan as an official partner of Expo 2025 Osaka, Kansai, Japan. This collaboration marks a major milestone in the brand's comeback.

During our time away, we took the opportunity to reformulate our Classic Peppermint mints. They're now sugar-free and Non-GMO Project Verified, delivering the same premium quality with even cleaner ingredients.

Known for our iconic curved tins and design-driven mints, Hint Mint is also partnering with Yodobashi Camera, Japan's leading electronics retailer. A limited-edition Expo 2025 tin will be available online and at select stores for a short time.

A limited-edition Expo 2025 tin will be available online for a limited time.

These collector tins are limited in quantity and won't be restocked — once they're gone, they're gone for good. Don't wait to grab yours!

With a history stretching back over 170 years, the World Expo has always been a global showcase for innovation, creativity, and culture — from the debut of the telephone at the 1876 Philadelphia Expo to today's cutting-edge breakthroughs.
