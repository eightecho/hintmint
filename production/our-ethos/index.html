Our Promise — Pure and Simple

100% Sugar Free . Dairy Free . Gluten Free . Vegan . Non GMO Certified

At Hint Mint, our promise is pure and simple: to craft premium mints that deliver an exceptional experience without compromise. Every mint is 100% sugar-free, dairy-free, gluten-free, vegan, and Non-GMO Project Verified. We believe that luxury should never require you to sacrifice your values or your well-being.

Our Values

Sustainability

We are committed to sustainability in every aspect of our business. Our iconic tins are designed to be recyclable and reusable, reducing waste and encouraging mindful consumption. From sourcing our ingredients responsibly to minimizing our environmental footprint, sustainability is woven into the fabric of everything we do.

Giving Back

Hint Mint believes in making a positive impact beyond our products. We proudly partner with charitable organizations, supporting causes that align with our values. From health awareness campaigns to community initiatives, we are dedicated to giving back and making a difference, one mint at a time.

Authenticity

Authenticity is at the heart of Hint Mint. Each mint is crafted using our patented leaf embossing press, ensuring that every product is genuinely ours — original, distinctive, and impossible to replicate. We stand behind the quality and integrity of our brand, delivering an authentic luxury experience with every tin.
