The Original Designer Mint

Since 1999, Hint Mint has set the standard for luxury mints — elevating the everyday with timeless design and unparalleled quality. Our signature curved tin and elegantly embossed mints transformed the market, making us the go-to mint for those who appreciate both style and substance.

Crafted with care, our mints are Non-GMO, vegan, and free from artificial flavors or colors — because true luxury should be both refined and responsible.

Note from the new owner

After working with Hint Mint for over 20 years, I'm excited to take over this iconic brand and continue its legacy while elevating it to new heights. My initial goal was simple: to create the perfect mint product.

The iconic Hint Mint Classic Tin, with its patented curved design and timeless artwork is always stylish and a constant classic. I did not want to change this impactful design. Instead, I decided to focus on the mint itself. When Hint Mint first debuted 25 years ago, it was unique for using sugar as its base. But as we've learned more about the health impacts of sugar, it was time to evolve.

I set out to create a sugar-free, Non-GMO mint made with the fewest ingredients possible. The result? A pure, refreshing peppermint mint made with just three simple ingredients.

Housed in our Classic designer tin, you'll find a refreshed, exceptional Classic Peppermint Mint. Join me as we celebrate Hint Mint's past and embark on an exciting journey to shape its future.

Erik
